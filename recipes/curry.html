Curry

Description

This recipe is the jamaican version of curry. It comes from the island of Jamaica in the West Indies.

Ingredients

  • curry
  • potatoes
  • pepper
  • chicken
  • ginger
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. In a pot on medium heat, add olive oil
  2. Add cut up ginger and let sizzle in oil, so oil soaks up flavor
  3. Add scotch bonnet pepper (cut in half for more spice)
  4. Add curry powder to oil
  5. Add seasoned chicken (overnight with salt and pepper) to oil
  6. Stir chicken until it becomes browned with curry
  7. Cover pot and let cook for 20 minutes
  8. Add cut up potatoes
  9. Cook until potatoes are soft
