Liver

Description

This recipe is the Japanese version of liver with ginger.

Ingredients

  • chicken liver
  • ginger
  • soy sauce
  • mirin
  • sugarr
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. In a pot on medium heat, add olive oil
  2. Add cut up ginger and let sizzle in oil, so oil soaks up flavor
  3. Add soy sauce and mirin
  4. Add sugar until it dissolves
  5. Add liver and stir in mix for a few minutes
  6. Cover pot and let liver cook
